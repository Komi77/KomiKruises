The Suzuki Alto is a compact car known for its efficient and practical design, making it well-suited for urban commuting and city driving. It typically features a small yet economical engine, good fuel efficiency, and a modest interior space. The Alto is often chosen for its affordability, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious buyers. The price range for a new Suzuki Alto was generally around $10,000 to $15,000 USD.

The Toyota Corolla is a well-established and popular compact sedan known for its reliability, comfort, and practicality. It's recognized for its efficient fuel economy, comfortable ride, and a reputation for being a durable vehicle. The Corolla typically offers a range of modern features and technology options, making it a versatile choice for a wide range of drivers. As for the price,the starting price for a new Toyota Corolla is typically around $20,000 to $25,000 USD.

The Toyota Hilux Revo, often referred to as the Toyota Hilux, is a rugged and versatile pickup truck known for its durability, off-road capabilities, and practicality. The Hilux Revo is renowned for its strong build quality, reliable performance, and a reputation for being a workhorse that can withstand demanding conditions.Regarding the price, the starting price for a new Toyota Hilux Revo is typically around $30,000 to $40,000 USD
